About us

Welcome to Yamada, the motorcycle brand that well-known for its classic yet classy shape. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of motorcycle, with a focus on excellence user experience. Founded in 2020 by Gerrald, Yamada has come a long way from Indonesia. When Gerrald first started out, his passion for motorcycle, creating the best and comfortable motorcycle, drove him to create a classic look yet classy and firm look, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming brand for motorcycle. We now serve customers all over South East Asia, and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the motorcycle industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Yamada
